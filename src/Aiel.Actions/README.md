# Aiel.Actions

This library provides a set of actions that can be performed on an Aiel character. These actions include:

- Attack: Perform an attack on an enemy.
- Buff: Apply a buff to yourself or an ally.
- Cast Spell: Cast a spell to deal damage or provide support.
- Charge: Charge towards an enemy to close the distance and attack.
- Counter: Counter an enemy's attack to deal damage back to them.
- Debuff: Apply a debuff to an enemy.
- Defend: Defend against an incoming attack.
- Dispel: Dispel a negative effect from yourself or an ally.
- Flee: Attempt to flee from combat.
- Guard: Guard an ally to protect them from incoming attacks.
- Heal: Heal yourself or an ally.
- Inspire: Inspire your allies to increase their attack power and defense.
- Meditate: Meditate to restore your health and mana over time.
- Parry: Parry an enemy's attack to reduce the damage taken and counterattack.
- Rally: Rally your allies to boost their morale and increase their combat effectiveness.
- Silence: Silence an enemy to prevent them from using their abilities.
- Stealth: Enter stealth mode to avoid detection by enemies.
- Stun: Stun an enemy to prevent them from taking any actions for a short period of time.
- Summon: Summon a creature to assist you in battle. 
- Taunt: Taunt an enemy to draw their attention away from your allies.
- Teleport: Teleport to a different location on the battlefield.
- Use Item: Use an item from your inventory.
- Withdraw: Withdraw from combat to avoid taking damage.
- Whirlwind: Perform a whirlwind attack to hit multiple enemies at once.
- Wild Attack: Perform a wild attack that has a chance to hit multiple enemies or deal extra damage.
